GrowApp - Help

Purpose

This app helps you track and visualize changes in the appearance - the 'phenology' - of trees and other vegetation. The phenology of a particular tree at a certain location and a certain moment in time is stored in the GrowApp database by adding a photo taken with this app. The app presents a geographic map with the locations of all photos taken by all users. A photo can be viewed by tapping the corresponding photo location. If the photo location happens to be near your current location, you can add new photos of the same subject. The app then shows a semi-transparent version of the previous photo on top of the camera view so that camera and photo can be visually aligned. Once more than one photo of the same subject at the same location has been taken, the app automatically constructs time-lapsed versions of these photos. If enough photos are taken over sufficiently large intervals, the resulting animations should clearly show all aspects of the changing vegetation phenology over time.

User Interface

Map
Use pinch to zoom in and out, swipe to pan. Red rectangles indicate photo locations, tap to show photo. Tap photo to enlarge photo

menu Menu

Toggles popup-menu for additional features

Map layers

Toggles list of additional map layers.
 Start of Season
layer shows when the vegetation season begins
 Vegetation trend
layer shows how much vegetation has changed since last year.
Yesterday's temperature
displays the surface temperature (not air temperature) for yesterday where available

 Location tracking

When activated, the map stays automatically centered at the currently tracked location until user manually pans to a different location

 Take picture

Starts the camera view to take a picture. Not available in the web-site version (app only)

 Add an additional photo

Only available in previously taken photos at or near your current location. Click to add a new picture of the same subject. Added photos are inserted into an automatically created time lapse animation

Requirements

Currently this app should work well with newer Android (version 4.4+) devices with GPS and camera enabled. The app requires an available mobile internet connection.
